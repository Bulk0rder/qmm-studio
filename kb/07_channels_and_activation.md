# Channels & Activation

**Definition**: Where to find the people.

## The Bullseye Framework
1. **Outer Ring**: Brainstorm all 19 channels (SEO, Ads, Sales, Events...).
2. **Middle Ring**: Test 3 promising ones.
3. **Inner Ring**: Scale the 1 that works.

## Top Channels for Nigeria B2B
- **LinkedIn Outbound**: High trust.
- **WhatsApp**: The operating system of African business.
- **Events**: Physical trust building.

## Top Channels for Nigeria B2C
- **Instagram/Tiktok**: Visual discovery.
- **Micro-Influencers**: Higher trust than mega-stars.
