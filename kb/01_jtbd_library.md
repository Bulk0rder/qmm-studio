# JTBD Library (Jobs To Be Done)

**Definition**: A catalog of the fundamental progress customers are trying to make.

## Core Framework
"When [Situation], I want to [Motivation], so that I can [Outcome]."

## Common B2B Jobs (Nigeria/Africa Context)

### 1. The "Reduction of Anxiety" Job
- **When**: The relentless volatility of FX/Policy hits...
- **I want to**: Secure stability or predictability...
- **So that**: I don't lose my business or sanity this quarter.

### 2. The "Status & Signaling" Job
- **When**: Dealing with top-tier clients...
- **I want to**: Look bigger and more professional than I am...
- **So that**: I can command premium pricing and trust.

### 3. The "Compliance Shield" Job
- **When**: Regulators are tightening nets...
- **I want to**: Automate or outsource my risk...
- **So that**: I avoid fines and keep my license.

## How to Interview for JTBD
- **Don't ask**: "What do you want?"
- **Ask**: "What happened the *moment* you decided to look for a solution?"
- **Look for**: The "push" of the old way and the "pull" of the new way.
